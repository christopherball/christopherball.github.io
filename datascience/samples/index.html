Donor Prospecting Logistic Regression
While this example is honestly not that great, as it faces major class imbalance issues that aren't elegantly handled, it does show the general process and method of attack. See chapter 5 of "Machine Learning in R". One of the observations I had about this project was the grey area on what it means to produce an accurate model with class imbalance issues. You could be accurate for the majority and inaccurate for the minority, accurate for the minority and inaccurate for the majority, or somewhere in between. Good learning experience.
